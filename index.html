Insights & Reflection

See the patterns you'd otherwise miss.

Deeply notices recurring themes, shifts in mood, and quiet patterns across your entries — then reflects them back to you in plain language, not clinical jargon.

  • Weekly activity and mood overview
  • Recurring themes like work, family, health, or rest
  • Gentle AI reflections, clearly labeled as a starting point — never a diagnosis
Range Dashboard

Zoom out from a week to a season.

A single day can be noisy — one bad night doesn't tell you much. Drag your finger across several weeks on the activity strip and Deeply combines them into one view, so you can see the shape of a longer stretch of your life at a glance.

  • Compare how a rough month stacks up against a calmer one
  • Spot slow-building patterns a single entry would never reveal — like stress quietly climbing over several weeks before it becomes obvious
  • Get a combined theme and mood breakdown for exactly the period you choose, instead of scrolling back through dozens of entries by hand
  • Useful context to bring into a conversation with a friend, family member, or counselor — "here's how the last two months actually went," backed by your own words
